    Rules and Regulations for Riders

    General Conduct

    Respect All Players: Treat all members with respect, both within the VTC and the TruckersMP community.

    No Discrimination or Harassment: Any form of discrimination, harassment, or bullying will not be tolerated.

    Language: Use appropriate language in all VTC-related communications. No offensive or vulgar language.

    Follow the Chain of Command: Respect the hierarchy within the VTC. If you have an issue, report it to your direct superior.

    Driving Rules

    Follow TruckersMP Rules: All players must adhere to TruckersMP‚Äôs official rules at all times.

    Realistic Driving: Drive in a realistic and responsible manner. No reckless driving, speeding, or dangerous overtaking.

    Obey Traffic Laws: Follow all in-game traffic laws, including speed limits, traffic signals, and road signs.

    Convoy Conduct: When participating in convoys, maintain proper spacing, follow the convoy leader, and avoid overtaking unless instructed.

    Activity Requirements

    Minimum Activity: Members must log a certain number of miles/kilometers per week/month as determined by the VTC leadership.

    Event Participation: Attend a minimum number of VTC events or convoys each month unless prior notice is given.

    Leave of Absence: If you are unable to meet activity requirements for a period of time, notify your manager in advance.

    Communication

    Discord Use: Members are required to join the VTC‚Äôs Discord server for communication purposes.

    Announcements: Regularly check the announcements channel for important updates and instructions.

    Voice Communication: Use voice channels appropriately during convoys and events. Follow the convoy leader‚Äôs instructions.

    Vehicle Customization

    VTC Paint Scheme: Use the official VTC paint scheme and logo on your truck during official events and convoys.

    Approved Mods: Only use VTC-approved mods to ensure compatibility and consistency among members.

    No Inappropriate Customizations: Avoid any decals, skins, or accessories that could be deemed offensive or inappropriate.

    Reporting Issues

    Report Misconduct: If you witness any rule violations, report them to the VTC leadership with evidence (e.g., screenshots, videos).

    Dispute Resolution: Attempt to resolve minor disputes amicably. If necessary, escalate the issue through the proper channels.

    Feedback: Provide constructive feedback to help improve the VTC.
